GPT Image 2 handles structured educational visuals well — numbered step diagrams, species identification grids, and tabular comparison charts with consistent iconography and legible labels. The prompts below demonstrate three formats commonly used in editorial, educational, and scientific communication: a linear process infographic, a naturalist field guide page, and a side-by-side method comparison chart. Each prompt specifies the number of items, their content, and layout structure to give the model a clear compositional target.
