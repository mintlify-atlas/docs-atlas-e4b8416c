GPT Image 2 produces photorealistic images with strong command of camera optics, film grain, light temperature, and lens characteristics. To activate the most realistic output, include explicit photographic framing in your prompt — phrases like "photorealistic", "real photograph", or "taken on a real camera" signal the model to prioritize physical plausibility over painterly or stylized aesthetics. The three prompts below cover street documentary, environmental portraiture, and architectural photography.

<Note>
  Adding "photorealistic", "real photograph", or "taken on a real camera" to any of these prompts anchors the output in a documentary register and suppresses painterly or illustrative interpretations.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Naming the lens focal length and film stock (35mm film, 50mm lens; medium format digital back; 17mm wide angle) gives the model enough optical context to produce consistent depth of field, perspective distortion, and grain characteristics. Color temperature values like "3200K tungsten warmth" are more precise than adjectives like "warm light" and produce more consistent results across runs.
</Tip>
