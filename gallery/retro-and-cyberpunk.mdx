<Tip>
  Naming every character slot in a grid prompt — "glass-horn DJ, koi-scale bartender, moth-wing hacker" — forces the model to allocate space and design distinctly for each rather than repeating similar figures. For atmospheric scenes, describing the light sources separately (cold teal ambient, warm amber from distant structure) produces more physically coherent neon lighting than naming a general "cyberpunk palette".
</Tip>
