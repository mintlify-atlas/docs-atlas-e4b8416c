GPT Image 2 produces publication-quality product and food photography from text prompts alone. Describe lighting setup, surface, props, and camera details explicitly — the model responds well to commercial photography vocabulary like "three-point studio lighting", "shallow depth of field", and "editorial food magazine style".

  <Accordion title="Commercial product shot — water bottle">
    **Size:** portrait · **Quality:** high · **Source:** Curated

    ```text
    A photorealistic commercial product photograph of a matte black cylindrical water bottle on a
    white seamless backdrop. Studio three-point lighting: key light from upper right, fill from
    left, rim light outlining the bottle's edge. Condensation droplets on the surface, brand area
    left intentionally blank. Shot on medium format, retouched finish.
    ```

    ```bash
    gpt-image -p "A photorealistic commercial product photograph of a matte black cylindrical water bottle on a white seamless backdrop. Studio three-point lighting: key from upper right, fill from left, rim light on edge. Condensation droplets on surface, brand area blank. Medium format, retouched finish." --size portrait --quality high -f product-bottle.png
    ```
